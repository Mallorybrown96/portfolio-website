My Story

Based in Miami, FL. I love to spend time with my family, my dog, being outdoors, cooking, and attending local yoga classes. My interest in seeing how others solve problems made my decision to transition into UI an easy one. I enjoy the challenge of creating designs that best fit the needs of individuals, while at the same time, creating an aesthetically pleasing interface.

As a UI designer, with a strong background in hospitality, I am experienced in understanding and solving guests needs. I excel at creating design solutions and executing them to make a positive impact for the user and the business. My passion for digital design and interfaces grew during the last couple years. I pursued further studies to improve my expertise in branding, color theory, and overall design.

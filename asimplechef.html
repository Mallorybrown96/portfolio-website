A Simple Chef is a responsive web app that contains step by step instructions for each recipe. A Simple Chef gives helpful tips, organizational tool, and even provide the user with videos and pictures of the recipes so they can paint a clear picture in their mind as to what is ahead of them. Users will be able to save and share recipes as well as create and post recipes of their own.

Overview

Key Features

  • Save and share recipes: users are able to save and share recipes via social media, SMS, or through email
  • Step by step instructions: there are step by step instructions for all recipes that include measurements
  • List making: user will have the ability to create shopping lists by selecting ingredients from recipes and adding them to the list
  • Recipe videos: videos and images will provided with each dish so the user can visually see the step by step instructions
  • Explore: users will have access to an explore page that includes recommended recipes, substitutions, and meal plan suggestions

User Scenarios

  • People who are entirely new to cooking or are looking to advance their techniques
  • Users who are nervous to cook and need multi step formats to begin
  • Health conscious users who want to curate shopping lists
  • Users who would like to create meal plans for the week
  • Users who want to get organized in the kitchen

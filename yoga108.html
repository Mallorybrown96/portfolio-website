Yoga108 is a new app for individuals who want to learn yoga, share the practice, and easily access yoga classes from studios and virtual remote videos. Users will have the ability to pay for classes/memberships with their linked credit card.

Overview

Key Features

  • Ability to sign up for yoga classes
  • Practice yoga from home or remotely
  • Provide user with meditation guides/breathing exercises
  • Invite friends to join the practice and the ability to share accomplishments via SMS, social media, and more
  • Easily pay for a class/monthly subsciptions

User Scenarios

  • People who are entirely new to yoga and are looking to advance their practice
  • Individuals who are interested in trying different yoga studios but do not want to commit to a membership yet
  • Yogis who would like to practice yoga in class and from the comfort of their home
